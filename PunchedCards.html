I see myself as a practical and creative programmer. There’s something romantic in seeing those punched cards, used not long ago to make computers work. This is an always changing field, but I sometimes feel that as we add layers and layers of abstraction to what we know about computers we are loosing the point. Besides, those who forget their past are doomed to repeat their mistakes. That is why I wanted to represent that with the eternal beauty of the always recognizable punched cards that began the path that we follow today.
