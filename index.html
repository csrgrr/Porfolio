PROJECTS

OCR-PT-CT

This project will conceive, design, and develop a digital toolset to perform optical character recognition (OCR) of ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs on the Pyramid Texts (PT) and the Coffin Texts (CT) to provide a semi-automatic transcription of the original text into a standard code known as Manuel de Codage (MdC). Besides the technical challenge, the OCR-PT-CT project might enable researchers to search for textual and sign parallels much more efficiently. By the University of Alcala de Henares.
